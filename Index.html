Elevator Pitch

Desktop Survivors 98 is a chaotic bullet hell dungeon crawler that unfolds right on your Desktop. Take control of your cursor as you explore new rooms, battle relentless waves of enemies, and collect powerful new weapons. Your screen becomes the ultimate battleground—will you survive?

Description

In Desktop Survivors 98, players face off against endless waves of enemies while navigating randomly generated rooms, all within a classic PC gaming aesthetic. The game’s unique twist is that it’s played directly on your desktop, blending survivors-like gameplay with your everyday workspace. With numerous weapons, items, and cursor characters to acquire, no two playthroughs are the same.

Inspired by Vampire Survivors and Rusty’s Retirement, the game seamlessly overlays on your desktop, and lets you toggle it on and off anytime. Whether you’re taking a quick break or diving into an extended challenge, Desktop Survivors 98 delivers fast-paced action perfectly suited for any kind of player.

Key Features

  • Play Directly on Your Desktop: The action happens directly on your desktop, overlaying on top of anything you’re working on—play while multitasking!
  • Cursor Combat: Your cursor becomes the hero as you fend off waves of enemies with precise, fast-paced action.
  • Dangerous Enemy Waves: Face relentless hordes in a bullet hell frenzy, testing your mouse-dodging skills and endurance.
  • Variety of Weapons and Upgrades: Discover and equip powerful, quirky items, upgrading them as you progress.
  • Nostalgic Aesthetic: Relive the past with retro-inspired visuals that capture the charm of classic PC gaming.
  • Take a Break Anytime: Toggle the game on or off at any moment—step away, relax, and jump back into the action whenever you’re ready.
